FOIA / records template

A generic wording you can adapt for any region. Replace the parts in brackets with your details.

To: [Agency / Department Name]
Subject: Request for records related to [PROGRAM / NONPROFIT NAME]

This is a request for records related to the administration, oversight, and funding of [PROGRAM / NONPROFIT NAME] in [CITY / PROVINCE / STATE] for the period [DATE RANGE].

Please provide, in electronic form where possible:
1. All grant or contract award documents, including scopes of work and budgets, naming [PROGRAM / NONPROFIT NAME] as a recipient, subrecipient, or implementing partner.
2. Any monitoring, inspection, or site visit reports related to these awards, including notes on compliance, performance, or beneficiary verification.
3. Any audits, evaluations, or investigative findings that reference [PROGRAM / NONPROFIT NAME] or its key officers during the requested period.
4. Any notices of suspension, debarment, refund, clawback, or enforcement actions arising from these awards.

If any portion of this request is denied, please provide the specific legal basis for the denial and release all reasonably segregable non-exempt portions of records.

I am requesting a fee waiver (or reduction) as these records are sought in the public interest to shed light on the use of public funds and the delivery of services to low-income communities.

Sincerely,
[Your name or organization]
[Contact information]
